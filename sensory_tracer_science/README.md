## Foundational Axioms

The STS framework is built upon five non-negotiable axioms:

- **A1**: Information ≠ Energy, but both are bounded by conservation laws (Landauer's Principle, Noether's Theorem)
- **A2**: No tracer can exceed v ≤ c/n in any medium (Special Relativity)
- **A3**: Total entropy ΔS_total ≥ 0; local decreases must be compensated (2nd Law of Thermodynamics)
- **A4**: Measurement adds noise ≥ ℏ/2 (Heisenberg Uncertainty)
- **A5**: ATP hydrolysis ≤ 57 kJ/mol sets biological upper bound (Cellular Bioenergetics)

## Validation Protocol

Every STS system must pass a triple audit:
1. **Energy Audit**: Energy conservation within 1 femtojoule tolerance
2. **Information Balance**: Information preservation within 1% tolerance
3. **Causality Check**: Signal speed ≤ speed of light in medium (zero tolerance)
